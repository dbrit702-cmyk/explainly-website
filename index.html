FAQ

Common questions

Is Explainly just giving me the answers? ▾
No — that's exactly what Explainly is not. The 5-card flow walks you through the concept, context, steps, and a self-check quiz before the answer is even revealed. The whole point is that you understand the solution before you see it. Getting the answer without the understanding is useless on exam day.
What subjects does Explainly support? ▾
Math (algebra, geometry, calculus, statistics), all sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), humanities (History, Geography), English, and Essay Mode for written assignments. If it's on a worksheet, Explainly can teach it.
Does it work with handwritten notes? ▾
Yes. Explainly reads handwritten problems, typed text from textbooks, printed worksheets, whiteboard photos, and screenshots. Built to work in real classroom and study conditions.
How is this different from Photomath or Gauthmath? ▾
Photomath and Gauthmath show you the answer. Explainly teaches you the concept. We support every subject (not just math), calibrate to your grade level, offer Essay Mode, include Practice Mode for retention, and explain in 12 languages. We're a tutor — not a calculator.
Is it free? ▾
Explainly is free to download, and your first problem is free — so you can see exactly how the teaching breakdown works before you decide. Pro unlocks unlimited explanations, Practice Mode, and diagrams for $6.99/month or $44.99/year, and every Pro plan starts with a 7-day free trial. No account required to start.
Is Explainly available on iPhone? ▾
Currently Android only. iOS support is on the roadmap. The Android build covers the full feature set right now.
